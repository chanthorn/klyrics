## 1. Cambodian Rhyme Structure (សូរ្យសព្ទ - Soursapt)

Khmer poetry and songwriting rely heavily on internal and end-rhymes based on vowel sounds.

* **Vowel Harmony:** Khmer is rich in vowel sounds. Focus on the **nucleus vowel** for rhyming. Words ending in *-ey* (e.g., *dey*, *mey*, *kdey*) create a soft, flowing rhyme.
* **Rhythmic Patterns:** Traditional *Klon* (poetry) often uses 4 or 7-beat structures. Modern Khmer Pop typically follows 4/4 time signatures, with phrase lengths in multiples of 4, 8, or 16 bars.
* **Prompting Strategy for AI:**
    > "Write 4 lines of lyrics in Khmer using the ABAB rhyme scheme, focusing on words that end with the 'a' vowel sound (e.g., *snea*, *chea*, *mea*). Keep the rhythm suitable for a mid-tempo pop track."

## 4. Master Prompt Template
Copy and paste this template to generate high-quality KLyrics:

> **"Act as a professional Khmer songwriter. Write lyrics for a [Insert Mood: e.g., heartbroken/upbeat] song about [Insert Topic].**
> 
> **Guidelines:**
> 1. **Language:** Use a mix of poetic Khmer and modern colloquialisms.
> 2. **Structure:** Verse-PreChorus-Chorus-Verse-Chorus-Bridge-Chorus.
> 3. **Rhyme:** Ensure lines 2 and 4 of each stanza share the same final vowel sound.
> 4. **Phonetics:** Provide the Khmer script followed by the phonetic transcription in parentheses.
> 5. **Vibe:** Influence the tone by the style of [Insert Artist Name]."

## 6. Pro-Tips for Better Results
* **Syllable Stress:** Khmer is sesquisyllabic. Ensure the AI places emphasis on the final syllable of phrases for a natural flow.
* **Visual Pairing:** Pair your lyrics with visual prompts: *"Cinematic Khmer aesthetics, golden hour in Phnom Penh, soft lighting, mood: [Insert Song Mood]."*
